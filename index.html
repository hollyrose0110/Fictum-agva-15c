Welcome to Cornerstone Lutheran High School

Cornerstone Lutheran High School is a Christ-centered, college-preparatory school located in Eau Claire, WI. We educate the whole student — mind, body, and spirit — preparing young people to thoughtfully engage with the world and serve with compassion and integrity.

Our small class sizes encourage strong relationships between students and faculty, personalized attention, and a supportive community where students can grow academically, spiritually, and socially.
